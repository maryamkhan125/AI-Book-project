# Book Specification: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## 1. Book Title
Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: An AI-Native Textbook

---

## 2. Purpose
To provide a structured, AI-native learning path for understanding
how artificial intelligence operates in physical systems,
with a focus on humanoid robots.

---

## 3. Learning Outcomes
By the end of this book, learners will be able to:
- Explain Physical AI concepts
- Understand humanoid robot architecture
- Design AI-driven robotic behaviors
- Reason about human-robot collaboration
- Apply ethical principles to robotics systems

---
